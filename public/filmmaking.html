Filmmaking/ Videography

Click here to see my IMDB page

  • Features

    I have directed a total of five feature-length movies to date, none of which have had a budget of over a few thousand dollars. The first two were co-directing efforts, so they only partially count. The latter three were all very difficult accomplishments, but I'm proud of all of them. Baby Alligators is my proudest accomplishment, as it was the most difficult to execute and it defied many odds to be completed. I'm Not Mine is also a proud accomplishment, as it took me nearly three years to complete (five if you count my first attempt in making it years prior).

  • Shorts/ Sketches

    I walked before I could run, and I was making features before I knew what I was doing instead of being smarter and starting with short films. I only have a couple of actual short films to my name. That being said, I have directed, edited, and photographed dozens of comedic sketches. The best and most successful of these sketches was the first part of my darkly comic Roomies series, which won the 2008 Second City Student Film Festival. It would have premiered on Cartoon Network's site, but the 2008 writer's strike got in the way. I hope to direct more shorts in the future.

  • Music Videos

    I have directed, photographed, and edited dozens of independent, nothing-budgeted music videos for a number of independent bands. It's a format that I absolutely adore, as it allows me to be creative, experiment with styles, and push certain filmmaking rules to their limits. Music videos are the purest fun and the most creatively rewarding form of filmmaking I have attempted to date.

  • Commercials/ Ads

    I have directed dozens of low-budget, independently produced ads over the years. I have done ads for a few companies, but the most significant ad work I have done to date was for CATCO Theatre in Columbus, OH. I worked for CATCO as an independent contractor for two years. My ads were all published online and some also aired on local cable stations. I enjoy doing ad work and would love to pursue it further in the future.

  • Television

    I have virtually no experience with television, though I have attempted to produce a couple of independent pilots. My next film project in general will most likely be another independent pilot made from my festival-ranking script, Film Camp.

  • Documentaries

    I did a couple of documentary shorts many years back, but have very little experience with the format. I have a couple of ideas for subjects, and I hope to pursue them eventually.

  • Web Videos

    While I have published short sketches online and I once directed a pilot intended to be the start of a web series, I haven't otherwise produced any videos that are specifically designed for web use. I might be editing a series of instructional and/or product review videos down the road, so time will tell if this is a format with which I spend much time.
